Brand

Guidelines for representing CO3 ONE across all media. For press inquiries, reach us at support@co3.one.

Name

The company name is CO3 ONE. Always uppercase, always with a space. Never "Co3 One", "co3one", or "CO3ONE".

The stylized mark uses Co3 with a superscript 3.

The subsidiary product is miji (見慈). Always lowercase. Never capitalize.

The tagline is Constraint-aware artificial intelligence.
