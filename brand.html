Brand

Guidelines for representing CO3 ONE across all media. Always write "CO3 ONE" in uppercase with a space. The parent company is CO3 ONE. For press inquiries, reach us at support@co3.one.

Name

The company name is CO3 ONE. Always uppercase, always with a space between CO3 and ONE. Never "Co3 One", "co3one", or "CO3ONE".

The stylized mark uses Co3 with a superscript 3.

The subsidiary product is miji. Always lowercase. Never capitalize the M.

Logo

The primary mark is the Co3 gravity well. Use the circular mark for icon contexts and the horizontal lockup for full-width placements.


Minimum clear space: the height of the superscript 3 on all sides.
Never rotate, stretch, recolor, or add effects to the logo.
Always place on dark backgrounds. The logo is not designed for light contexts.
